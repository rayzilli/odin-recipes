Gold Rush Stew

"Our need will be the real creator" Plato

During the gold rush folks implemented inventive methods to cook up a tasty meal for there friends and family. Unusual as it may seem, Chaplin proved a well prepared right boot that could satisfy the most discriminating palate. Proper seasoning is the key to this recipe. A thanksgiving classic.

Ingredients

  • Right well worn boot
  • A large metal pot
  • Two tablespoons of salt

Steps

  1. Clean right shoe with sleeve of jacket.
  2. Add shoe to pot and cover with water.
  3. Add two tablespoons of salt and possibly more to taste.
